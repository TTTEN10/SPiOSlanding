# SafePsy Tokenomics — Revised Version (LLM-First Model)

**Canonical source** for $PSY token design. SafePsy is focused exclusively on the AI-powered mental health assistant (LLM therapist companion); no therapist marketplace. The token enhances the SaaS model and acts as the premium privacy and participation layer.

This document describes the **$PSY token** and its role in SafePsy’s business model, pricing, and profitability. For tier pricing and implementation status, see [PRICING_COMPLETE_GUIDE.md](PRICING_COMPLETE_GUIDE.md).

---

## 1. Business Model Alignment

SafePsy is focused exclusively on:

- **AI-powered mental health assistant** (LLM therapist companion)

No therapist marketplace (for now). This simplifies token logic and regulatory exposure.

**Revenue sources:**

- $19/month Premium  
- $69/month Gold  
- Corporate AI wellness subscriptions  
- API / enterprise privacy integrations (future)

The token **enhances** this SaaS model — it does not replace it.

---

## 2. Dual Payment Model (Core Structure)

SafePsy allows:

- **Fiat payments** (Stripe), or  
- **$PSY payments**

Fiat remains first-class to avoid friction. However, **$PSY unlocks structural advantages** — not just discounts.

### Utility Comparison

| Feature | Fiat | $PSY |
|--------|------|------|
| **Subscription** | Yes | Yes |
| **Discount** | — | **10%** |
| **AI Memory Vault** (encrypted long-term storage) | Limited | **Unlimited** |
| **Advanced Privacy Layer** (deep encryption features) | No | **Yes** |
| **Early Access to Experimental AI Features** | No | **Yes** |
| **Governance Participation** (Community Advisory) | No | **Yes** |

### Strategic Framing

**$PSY** is the **premium privacy and participation layer** of SafePsy.

---

## 3. Revenue-Sharing Model

**Mechanism:**

- **X = 9%** of platform net revenue → **Treasury**
- **Y = 3%** of Treasury → **Distributed to stakers**

This creates:

- Sustainable token demand  
- Real economic yield  
- Direct alignment with product success  

**Staking** = long-term belief in SafePsy growth.  
**Yield** derives from real subscriptions, enterprise contracts, and AI usage — not artificial APY.

---

## 4. Gold Tier Integration

**Gold** ($69/month) is the power-user tier.

Gold members receive:

- **10% cashback in $PSY** (credited to the user’s wallet)
- **Enhanced staking multiplier**
- **Early feature access**
- **Governance proposal rights**

This creates **recurring fiat → structured token demand** without forcing token dependency.

---

## 5. ICO Strategy (Product-Led, Metrics-Based)

### Phase 0 — Private Sale

- Limited allocation  
- Strategic investors only  
- Strict vesting schedule  
- Focus: runway + credibility  

### Phase 1 — Product-Led Pre-Sale

- **No governance weight bonus.**  
- Benefits: early Premium access, early access to privacy vault features, access to beta AI models, staking eligibility.  
- **Goal:** Token holders = early users, not pure speculators.

### Phase 2 — Public Sale (After Metrics = Credibility)

Public ICO launches **only after** reaching:

- **X = 20,000** active users  
- **Z = $100,000** Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)

## 6. Token Allocation (Revised)

**Total supply = fixed (no inflation).**

| Allocation | % |
|------------|---|
| Public Sale | 25% |
| Team & Advisors (3-year vesting, 1-year cliff) | 22% |
| Ecosystem Rewards | 21% |
| Treasury & Partnerships | 7% |
| Liquidity & Exchanges | 10% |
| User Growth Reserve | 10% |
| Foundation (Accessibility Mission) | 5% |

Long-term onboarding runway; less speculative volatility.

---

## 7. Airdrop Mechanics (Proof-of-Mental-Health Participation)

### Airdrop Score Formula

**Airdrop score** =

- (Holding score × **20%**)  
- + (Platform activity × **25%**)  
- + (Staking duration × **15%**)  
- + (Referral quality × **5%**)  
- + Remaining % dynamically adjusted based on: subscription longevity, AI session consistency, responsible usage metrics  

**Effects:**

- Real users benefit more  
- Passive farmers disincentivized  
- Token rewards aligned with mission  

**Distribution:**

- **20%** of total supply from Ecosystem pool  
- Released over **3 years**  

---

## 8. Deflationary Mechanism

To strengthen long-term scarcity: **Buyback + Burn**.

**Mechanism:**

- **5%** of platform net revenue used to **buy $PSY from market**  
- Purchased tokens **permanently burned**  

**Effects:**

- Continuous supply reduction  
- Confidence signal  
- Long-term holder incentive  

**No inflation. No artificial APY. Real economic loop.**
