**Template types**
- **Warm intro** — Referred; mutual connection; they know the referrer.
- **Semi-warm** — They’ve engaged (content, event, community); light prior touchpoint.
- **Cold** — No prior connection; lead with value and clarity.

- **Compliance:** Use only approved wording from CLAIMS_AND_TRUST_LANGUAGE; no “HIPAA certified,” “E2E encrypted,” or “replaces therapy.”
- **Customize:** Drop in [Referrer], [Name], [event], [program], and [Amount]/[use] for investors.
