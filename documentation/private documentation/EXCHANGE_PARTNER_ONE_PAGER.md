## What SafePsy is

SafePsy is a privacy-first mental wellness platform. Users connect a Web3 wallet, create an on-chain identity (DID, soulbound, currently Sepolia), and chat with Dr. Safe—an ethical AI assistant for reflection and support between therapy sessions. Data is encrypted at rest; encryption keys are derived from the user’s wallet and are not stored on SafePsy servers. We do not use chat content to train models or store user data with third parties. The AI does not replace licensed therapy: it does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Monetization includes freemium, premium, and $PSY token for premium and ecosystem. Governance: Timelock + multisig for contract upgrades and sensitive operations.

## Why your users care

- **Wallet-native:** One sign-in (wallet); no separate email/password account. Identity and keys stay in user control—no central custody of sensitive data.
- **Real utility:** Mental wellness support with clear boundaries (complement to therapy, not a therapist). Appeals to users who care about privacy and data ownership.
- **Token alignment:** $PSY ties into product and ecosystem; users who hold or earn can access premium or participate in the ecosystem.
- **Differentiation:** “No training on your data” and user-controlled encryption are strong messages for privacy-conscious and crypto-native audiences.

## Compliance posture / disclaimers (careful wording)

Use this wording in partner materials, listings, and campaigns. Do **not** use the disallowed phrases in [CLAIMS_AND_TRUST_LANGUAGE.md](../public documentation/CLAIMS_AND_TRUST_LANGUAGE.md).

- **What SafePsy is:** “Privacy-first mental wellness platform with wallet-based identity (DID) and an ethical AI assistant (Dr. Safe) for support between therapy sessions. Data encrypted at rest; keys controlled by user; we do not use chat content to train models or store user data with third parties.”
- **What SafePsy is not:** “SafePsy is not a substitute for professional mental health care. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult licensed professionals for clinical decisions. Not HIPAA certified.”
- **Token / listing:** “[If applicable] $PSY is a utility token for premium access and ecosystem participation. This is not financial or medical advice. Users must read our Terms of Service and applicable disclaimers.”
- **Network:** “DID operations currently run on Sepolia Testnet. Check the app and docs for the current network.”
- **Availability:** “We aim for high availability but do not guarantee uninterrupted service. AI and infrastructure may be modified or suspended as described in our Terms.”

Partner should ensure its own listing and marketing comply with local rules (securities, advertising, health claims). SafePsy provides the above wording; partner is responsible for its use and any additional disclaimers required in its jurisdiction.
